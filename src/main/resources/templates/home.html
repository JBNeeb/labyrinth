labyrinth

labyrinth is a multiplayer board game for 2-4 players. the players have to reach the symboles matching to their cards
they have gotten. how many cards there are is dependent from the number of players playing. after having standed on all
cards the player had to reach, the player has to get back to its startingpoint (one of the corners of the board).
the winner is the player who first visited all the positions he needed and stands on his starting point again.
